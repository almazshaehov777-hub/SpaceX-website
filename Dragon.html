Dragon features

Load capacity

Dragon is capable of delivering up to 6,000 kg into orbit and returning up to 3,000 kg to Earth.

Engines

Dragon uses 16 Draco thrusters for orbit maneuvering and orientation

The Dragon spacecraft is designed to deliver crew and cargo to the International Space Station (ISS) and return them to Earth.
