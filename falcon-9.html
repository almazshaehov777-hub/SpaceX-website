Falcon-9 features

reusable

The first stage of the Falcon 9 can be reused, which significantly reduces the cost of launches.

Engines

Uses 9 "Merlin 1D" engines, which provides high power and efficiency.

Landing technology

The rocket can land on land platforms and offshore platforms, which simplifies the launch process.

The Falcon 9 is a two—stage launch vehicle designed for reusable use of the first stage. It can carry up to 22,800 kg of payload into low Earth orbit and is used to launch commercial satellites, scientific research vehicles and the Dragon 2 cargo spacecraft.
